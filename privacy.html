Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026
TACTICO is a single-player, offline football-management game. There is no account and no login. Your career/save data is stored only on your device. The only data that leaves your device is what Google AdMob uses to serve and measure the optional rewarded video ads you choose to watch.

Who we are

This app ("TACTICO") is published by Emin Kaya. For any privacy question, contact eminkaya1903@hotmail.com.

Data we collect ourselves

None that identifies you. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, or game progress. Save games live in on-device storage and are removed when you delete the app.

Data collected by our advertising partner (Google AdMob)

TACTICO shows optional rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. When you choose to watch one, Google may collect and process, as an independent controller:

  • a device advertising identifier (IDFA) and similar identifiers;
  • IP address and coarse location derived from it;
  • ad-interaction and diagnostic data (impressions, clicks, crashes).

Google uses this to deliver and measure ads, prevent fraud, and (with your consent) personalise ads. See Google's policy at policies.google.com/privacy and how Google uses information from apps that use its services.

Your choices & tracking

On first launch (iOS 14.5+) we show Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you choose "Ask App Not to Track", the advertising identifier is not used to track you across apps and ads become non-personalised. The game is fully playable either way — ads are never required. In the EEA/UK we also show Google's consent (UMP) form. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

Children

TACTICO is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Data retention & deletion

We hold no personal data to retain. Local save data is deleted when you delete the app. For AdMob-held data, use the Google links above.

In-app purchases

There are none. No subscriptions, no "remove ads", no paid tier. Monetisation is optional rewarded video only.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change, and material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
