Frequently asked

Do I need an account to play?

No. TACTICO is single-player and offline — tap CONTINUE or NEW CAREER and play. There is no sign-up or login.

Where is my save stored? Can I lose it?

Your career is saved automatically on your device. Deleting the app deletes the save. There are multiple save slots so you can run more than one career.

Are the ads required? Is there pay-to-win?

No to both. Rewarded video ads are entirely optional — you only ever see one if you choose to watch it for a small bonus, and ads never sell power or gate the game. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions or paywalls.

It asked permission to track — do I have to allow it?

No. The App Tracking Transparency prompt is optional. Choose "Ask App Not to Track" and the game still plays fully; ads simply become non-personalised.

Are the clubs and players real?

No — every club, player, league and competition in TACTICO is fictional and original.
